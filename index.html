How To Play

Basics

Rotting Roulette Is A Game Where The Players Try To Kill All Other Players By Shooting Them

Each Player Has 3 HP

During Your Turn You Choose Who To Shoot

When You Choose Who To Shoot, There Will Be Three Outcomes

Blank - Nothing Happens

Live - They Lose 1 HP

Alcohol - They Get An Alcohol

Alcohol

When A Bullet Is Shot By You Or An Opponent It Has A Chance Of Being Alcohol, This Applies To Shooting Yourself Too.

For Information On A Type Of Alcohol, Click On The Alcohol In Your Inventory
